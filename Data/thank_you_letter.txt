Here is an example of a thank-you letter:

**Formal Thank-You Letter**

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I wanted to take a moment to express my heartfelt gratitude for [specific action or gift]. Your [generosity/support/kindness] has made a significant impact on me, and I am truly grateful for your presence in my life.

Your [action/gift] has [briefly describe the positive impact it has had on you]. I am thrilled to have [briefly mention what you are looking forward to doing with the gift or how it has helped you].

Please know that your thoughtfulness has not gone unnoticed, and I am so appreciative of your consideration. You have a special place in my heart, and I am grateful for our [relationship/friendship/acquaintance].

Once again, thank you for your kindness and generosity. I am so grateful to have you in my life.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

**Informal Thank-You Letter**

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I just wanted to say thanks a million for [specific action or gift]! It really made my day/week/month, and I'm so grateful for your thoughtfulness.

Your [action/gift] has been a huge help to me, and I'm really looking forward to [briefly mention what you are looking forward to doing with the gift or how it has helped you].

Thanks again for being such an amazing [friend/family member/acquaintance]. I'm so lucky to have you in my life.

Take care,

[Your Name]

**Email Thank-You Note**

Subject: Thank You for Your [Gift/Support]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I wanted to shoot you a quick email to say thanks so much for [specific action or gift]. It really means a lot to me, and I'm so grateful for your kindness.

Thanks again for your thoughtfulness. I'm looking forward to [briefly mention what you are looking forward to doing with the gift or how it has helped you].

Best,

[Your Name]

Remember to customize the letter according to your needs and relationship with the recipient. A sincere and heartfelt thank-you note can go a long way in nurturing relationships and showing appreciation for someone's kindness.
