Here is a sample application for sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, ZIP]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, ZIP]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a sick leave from [start date] to [end date] as I am currently unwell and unable to attend work.

Due to [briefly mention the reason for your illness, e.g. fever, illness, injury, etc.], I am under doctor's orders to take [number] days of rest and recover at home. I am not feeling well enough to perform my duties and require this time off to recuperate and regain my health.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I will provide a medical certificate from my doctor, if required.

I would greatly appreciate it if you could approve my request for sick leave. If there is any additional information required, please let me know.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that you should customize this letter as per your needs and the company's policies.
